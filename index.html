Vicente Helano

I currently work at Universidade Federal do Cariri in Juazeiro do Norte, Brazil. I am in charge of courses on numerical methods.

I received a D.Sc. degree in Civil Engineering from Federal University of Rio de Janeiro in 2010, with thesis entitled "Triangle Transversals and Their Applications to Triangulations". Since then, I have switched to Computer Science.

My research interests are concentrated in the field of computational geometry and its applications. I enjoy working on the design and analysis of algorithms and data structures for problems dealing with geometric data, parallel algorithms and numerical methods.
